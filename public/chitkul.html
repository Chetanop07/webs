Chitkul

Chitkul is a scenic village in Himachal Pradesh's Kinnaur district, famous as the last inhabited place on the Indo-Tibetan border (near the Indo-China border). Situated in the Baspa Valley at an altitude of 3,450 meters (11,320 feet), it is known for its stunning natural beauty, clean air, traditional Himalayan architecture, and the beautiful Baspa River.
