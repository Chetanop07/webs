Dalhousie

🌲 About Dalhousie

Dalhousie is a peaceful hill station in Himachal Pradesh known for its colonial-era architecture, pine forests, and scenic mountain views. It is perfect for relaxation and nature walks.

📍 Top Attractions

  • Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Panchpula Waterfall
  • Khajjiar (Mini Switzerland of India)
  • St. John's Church

🌤️ Best Time to Visit

March to June (pleasant weather) & December (snowfall experience).

💡 Travel Tips

Carry warm clothes, comfortable shoes, and book hotels in advance during peak season.
