Triund

Triund is a popular, relatively easy 9 km trekking destination near McLeodganj in Himachal Pradesh, situated at roughly 2,875 meters (9,429 feet). Known as the "crown jewel of Dharamshala," it offers stunning views of the Dhauladhar range and Kangra Valley, making it ideal for camping, beginners, and weekend trips.
