About Kasol

Kasol is a beautiful village in Parvati Valley, known for its scenic beauty, riverside camping, trekking routes like Kheerganga, and vibrant cafe culture. Location: Situated in the Parvati Valley, Himachal Pradesh, on the banks of the Parvati River, roughly 36 km from Kullu. Vibe: Known for its relaxed "hippie" culture, a unique blend of Indian and Israeli influences, and many cafés. Best Time to Visit: March to June (pleasant weather) or October to February (for cold, potential snowfall). Key Attractions: Trekking & Hiking: Starting point for treks like Kheerganga, Grahan, and Rasol. Chalal Village: A short, easy hike from Kasol for riverside views. Manikaran Sahib: Famous for hot springs and a gurudwara (approx. 4 km away). Cafes: Famous for authentic Israeli dishes, pizzas, and coffee. How to Reach: Best accessed by road from Bhuntar (nearest airport) or by bus from Delhi/Chandigarh via Bhuntar.
