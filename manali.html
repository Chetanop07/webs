About Manali

Manali is a popular hill station in Himachal Pradesh's Kullu Valley, located at an altitude of 1,929 meters. Known for its scenic snow-capped peaks, pine forests, and the Beas River, it serves as a major tourist destination for adventure sports (paragliding, skiing) and a gateway to Lahaul and Ladakh via the Rohtang Pass. Location: Situated in the northern end of the Kullu Valley in the Himalayas. Best Time to Visit: December to February for snow and skiing, and March to June for pleasant weather.

Top Attractions

  • Solang Valley
  • Rohtang Pass
  • Hadimba Temple
  • Old Manali Cafes
